Tearsheet icons

Tearshees act as a universal companion icon to upcoming Meetups. They contain the date of the event, but should never be relied on as the only conveyance of the event date.

.tearsheet-25, .tearsheet, .tearsheet-80

Oct 25 Oct ## Oct 80 
 <time class="tearsheet-25">
	<span class="month">Oct</span>
	<span class="day">25</span>
</time>

Figure set

Figure sets are a common visual pattern on Meetup that consist of a figure (ie. an image) to the left of some descriptive text.

.figureset-right is a variation of .figureset that puts the figure on the right.
